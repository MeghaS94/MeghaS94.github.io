Simulating Sparks

Wrote a physics based sparks simulator using PyOpenGL. This was my submission to the all India competition - Dreamworks FX Challenge 2016. I was one of the top 6 finalists.

The solver is based on the principles of newtonion physics, with features like splitting and bouncing of the sparks upon hitting the ground, collision detection with complex objects like the stanford bunny. Used the bill boarding technique to render the sparks.
